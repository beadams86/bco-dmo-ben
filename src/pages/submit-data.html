Can I Submit Data to BCO-DMO?

You should contribute data to BCO-DMO if your project is funded by NSF OCE's Biological or Chemical Oceanography Sections or the Division of Polar Programs' Antarctic Organisms & Ecosystems Program.

BCO-DMO provides data management services at no additional cost to projects funded by NSF's Division of Ocean Sciences' Biological and Chemical Oceanography Sections and the Division of Polar Programs' Antarctic Organisms & Ecosystems Program.

BCO-DMO staff members work with researchers funded by these NSF programs to ensure that data and metadata contributed to BCO-DMO are in compliance with the current NSF OCE Sample and Data Policy (NSF 17-037). To contribute data to BCO-DMO, follow the 5 steps from this page, starting with Register Project.

Submit Data & Forms

Once you've completed the previous steps you should be ready to upload your metadata forms and data files.

You will receive a reply from one of our Data Managers confirming receipt. Please allow up to 1 month for processing time.

Or if you'd prefer, email metadata forms and data files to info@bco-dmo.org.

Collaborate

Once you've submitted your data and necessary forms a Data Manager will begin the process of making the data available online.

We strive to develop robust metadata that will ensure the data are easily discoverable and reusable.

Your Data Manager will contact you with follow-up questions or requests for more information to ensure that the metadata is complete. This may be an iterative process, so your patience and cooperation are greatly appreciated.

Validate

Once your datasets are online, you’ll be asked to review the data and metadata for completeness and accuracy. This validation stage is the final step in the process, and necessary for assignment of DOIs and long-term archive.
